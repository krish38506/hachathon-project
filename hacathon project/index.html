How to prevent malaria

Malaria can be prevented by using mosquito nets, applying insect repellent, and taking antimalarial medications as prescribed.

How to prevent fever

Fever can be prevented by maintaining good hygiene, getting vaccinated, and avoiding exposure to sick individuals.

How to prevent chickenpox

Chickenpox can be prevented by getting vaccinated and avoiding close contact with infected individuals.

How to avoid obesity

Obesity can be avoided by maintaining a balanced diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding excessive consumption of high-calorie foods.
